About this Project

Hi,

Thank you for your visit!
Generally speaking, this website could predict the range of salary for a Data Analyst job base on the skills that required.
It also tell you what skills you might need to learn to boost your salary.
The predicting model is XGBoost, a Machine Learning model that deployed by Flask and Python.
Every time your click the submit button, the serve would run the Machine Learning and that is why it is slow.
